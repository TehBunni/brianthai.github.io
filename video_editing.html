My Musical & Artistic
Fulfillment

While I played piano and the clarinet during my early childhood, I was never satisfied
with my skills as its growth felt stagnant. This lead to a loss in motivation to pursue music as a whole.
Looking to fill the hole in my heart, I found video editing to be my forte. Ever since
creating my YouTube Channel, TehBunni, I was able to express my musical side through sync and VFX.
